About Studio V

Studio V is a family-owned premium clothing brand proudly based in Bulacan, Philippines. Since our founding in 2025, we have been on a mission to redefine modern fashion by blending timeless elegance with everyday comfort and a deep sense of purpose.

Our Story

  • The Meaning of "V": The “V” in Studio V stands for “5J”, representing the five members of our family—all sharing the first initial J.
  • Family Inspiration: The brand is inspired by our youngest, Vivienne, whose creativity and spirit shape our vision.
  • Rooted in Community: We are deeply connected to our Bulacan roots, collaborating with local artisans and supporting our community.

What We Stand For

  • Ethical Sourcing & Fair Labor
  • Sustainable Materials for a healthier planet
  • Intentional Design that reflects authenticity
  • Family, Community, and Conscious Living

Our Mission

  • To help you express your individuality with confidence and comfort.
  • To create clothing and accessories that are stylish, soulful, and responsibly made.
  • To make style more personal, responsible, and inspiring.
“Studio V isn't just about fashion—it's about family, community, and conscious living. Join us on this journey to make style more meaningful.”
